# SSH Identity Notice

**Created:** 2026-07-15  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-17

Live identity YAML files are intentionally not stored in this repository. They contain device-specific SSH public keys, fingerprints, identity labels, and approved target allowlists, and are maintained privately by the operator.

Use `_new-device-template.yml.example` to understand the schema without exposing or deploying a real identity.
