# Operations

**Created:** 2026-07-09  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-20

Operations holds records that cross more than one platform or infrastructure owner:

- `Inventory/` - dated current-state and historical inventories
- `Maintenance/` - cross-system maintenance records
- `Diagnostics/` - raw diagnostic output such as SMART results

Service changes & troubleshooting stay with their owner. This directory keeps the shared inventory, maintenance history, & raw diagnostic output that those records cite.
